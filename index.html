What is the London Game Space?

The London Game Space is a games-related shared working and events space in Greater London, formed in 2014.

Opening hours & events

The London Game Space is currently open Monday to Friday, 10:30am to 6pm.

Where is it?

The London Game Space is located in the basement of the Scenario Bar, 97 Stoke Newington Road, Dalston, London N16 8BX.

It's about 10 minutes walk from Dalston Junction and Dalston Kingsland Overground stations. Other ways to get there on public transport.

How do I get involved?

You can join London Game Space Discussion on Google Groups. New posters are moderated to prevent spam, so don't worry if your first email doesn't show up immediately.

We're also on Twitter @LondonGameSpace.
